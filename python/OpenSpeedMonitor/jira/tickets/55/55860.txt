Output format JPEG and PNG	"*Summary*

Create a possibility to download the full diagram (diagram incl. legend) in the JPEG and PNG format.



*Affected JS-Files*

* ifdController

* ifdTemplate

* ifdContentController

* ifdLegendController

* ifdContentMain

* ifdLegendMain



*Approach*

Use the GoJS-functionality ""makeImage"" ( http://gojs.net/latest/api/symbols/Diagram.html#makeImage ). Combine ""both"" GoJS-diagrams (diagram and legend) into one and create a JPEG-file or PNG-File out of that.

Use ITERAPLAN-3764 as blueprint.



*UI:*

Include the functionality as new subitem in the Download-Button of the IFD."		New Feature	iteraplan PO	02/Sep/15 2:06 PM	10/Sep/15 4:12 PM					download	ifd	jpeg
